# case_4_human_review

## Input
Customer says: I think someone hacked my account and made purchases.

## Expected
Escalate to human support and provide safety steps.

## Model Output
**Subject:** Security Concern: Potential Account Compromise

**Summary:**
The customer reported a suspected security breach involving their account, noting that unauthorized purchases have been made.

**Key Points:**
*   **Issue:** Potential account takeover/hacking.
*   **Impact:** Unauthorized financial transactions.

**Action Items:**
*   Verify account ownership and secure the account (e.g., password reset, enable MFA).
*   Review transaction history to identify specific unauthorized charges.
*   Initiate the dispute/refund process for fraudulent purchases.
